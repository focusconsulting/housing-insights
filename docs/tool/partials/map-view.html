Who is this for?

This data tool is designed for affordable housing advocates and government officials to better understand the state of affordable housing in Washington, DC.

What can it do?

The tool provides a dynamic view of all affordable housing in the city. You can filter the projects to view summary statistics of projects that meet certain criteria, and view neighborhood data overlayed on top of the map. You can also view details of specific projects.

Where is the data from?

Data has been gathered from multiple sources including the DC Government, Urban Institue, HUD, and the US Census Bureau. A full listing of data sources is located on our data documentation page
